About Us

Talk It Out was founded in the year 2019 as a social enterprise.Our passion in helping the youth, Marginalised women and girls navigate through life challenges was the fuelling factor .Mental health wellness, sexual reproductive health and rights education(SRHR), life skills training are our key areas of performance .We have stepped in the gap to help reduce and attend to an otherwise worrying rising numbers of mental conditions among the youth, and provide a safe space for information access on Sexual Reproductive Health and Rights.Our services and programs are carefully selected and executed in honor of human dignity, kindness,love and wellbeing. We at TALK IT OUT (TIO) commit to walk the journey with you until the storm is over .
